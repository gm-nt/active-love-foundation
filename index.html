About Active Love Foundation

Founded in 2023 and incorporated in 2024, Active Love Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization headquartered in Langata, Nairobi. Our foundation was born from the personal experience of our founder, Rose Odindo, who witnessed firsthand how financial barriers can derail educational journeys.

We focus on supporting junior secondary school students in Langata and Siaya, providing crucial assistance to ensure access to education. Our work is guided by core values of Compassion, Integrity, Empowerment, Equity, and Community Ownership.

Our Mission

To empower bright girls from impoverished backgrounds by removing barriers to education and providing holistic support for their academic and personal development.

Our Values

  • ✓ Compassion in all our actions
  • ✓ Integrity in our operations
  • ✓ Empowerment through education
  • ✓ Equity for all beneficiaries
  • ✓ Community Ownership

Our Projects: Project Msichana Shuleni

Project Msichana Shuleni ("Girls in School") is our flagship initiative designed to empower bright, impoverished girls aged 13-21 in the Nyanza and Nairobi regions of Kenya.

Scholarship Facilitation

We cover administrative costs for scholarship applications and provide comprehensive guidance through complex processes, including support for tests like SATs.

Safe Houses

We operate safe houses in Nairobi and Kisumu that provide secure residential facilities for girls during holidays and transition periods.

Daily Feeding Program

We provide daily nutritious meals for 100 high school girls during school terms to ensure consistent nutrition and improved concentration.

Digital Platform

We're developing a user-friendly platform for girls to express their needs, access resources, and connect with the foundation.

Transforming Lives Through Education

Active Love Foundation is proud of the tangible impact we've made in the lives of bright, deserving girls in Kenya.

Ivy League Success

Submit a Potential Beneficiary Case

If you know a girl aged 13-21 from an impoverished family in the Nyanza or Nairobi regions who is excelling academically but faces significant barriers to continuing her education, we encourage you to submit her case for consideration.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 13 and 21 years
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Enrollment or intention to enroll in secondary school or university
  • Commitment to education and project activities
